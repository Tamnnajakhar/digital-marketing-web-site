Frequently Asked Questions

What is Social Media Advertising?>

It involves paying platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn to display your content to specific audiences, guaranteeing reach and driving immediate traffic.

What is the difference between ads and boosted posts?>

Boosted posts are simple promotions for engagement. Ads are created in Ads Manager with advanced targeting, objectives (like sales/leads), and formatting options not available to boosted posts.

How much should I spend on ads?>

This depends on your goals, but we generally recommend a starting budget of at least $1,000/month in ad spend to gather enough data for optimization.

Which platforms are best for me?>

B2B businesses often thrive on LinkedIn, while B2C e-commerce brands see great success on Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok. We help you decide based on your audience.
